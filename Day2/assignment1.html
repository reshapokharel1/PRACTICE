Empowering Business and Technology at Kathmandu University

About BBIS

The Bachlore in Business Information System(BBIS) program at Kathmandu University combines the knowledge of business management and Information technology to create professionals who can bridge the gap between business and IT.

About IS Club

The Information System Club is a student-led club under BBIS that promotes learning and innovation in the fields of technology, data, business systems, and event organization. The club regularly hosts workshops, seminars, hackathons, and industrial visits

Our Activities

  • Workshops and Programming, Design, and Business Tools
  • Hackathon and Tech Competitions
  • Guest Lectures from Industry Experts
  • Field Visits and Industrial Tours
  • Networking and Community Events
